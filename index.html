INSTRUCTIONS
0. Click Ctrl and - together and change the size to 90.
1. Keep your laptop screen straight.
2. Move yourself approximately 2-3 feet away from the laptop.
3. Move your right wrist up and down. A red dot should appear on the wrist.
4. Now press the play button. and again move yourself 2-3 feet away from the laptop.
5. The red paddel is your paddel. It will move according to the movement of your wrist.
6. To reload, click on the red button at the bottom right.
